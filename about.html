Our story

Born in Surabaya, made with patience

Black Garlic Unti was created to make premium fermented black garlic approachable for Indonesian homes without losing the craft, taste, or trust that makes it special.

We believe a powerful superfood should taste good enough to become part of everyday life.

From local passion to daily wellness

Our process respects the simple beauty of garlic. With careful selection, precise fermentation, and small-batch attention, ordinary bulbs become dark, soft cloves with a naturally sweet umami profile. No shortcuts, no unnecessary additives, just time, warmth, and care.

The fermentation process

Over several weeks, controlled warmth and humidity gently transform fresh garlic into black garlic with a softer aroma, darker color, and layered flavor.

Select

Whole garlic bulbs are sorted for quality, size, and freshness.

Warm

Stable heat and humidity start the natural transformation.

Ferment

The bulbs slowly darken and develop mellow sweetness.

Finish

Each batch is checked for texture, aroma, and deep umami taste.
